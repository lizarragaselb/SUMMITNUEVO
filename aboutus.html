Summit's Three Quality Promises

Summit has conducted over 12,000 wine and spirits tastings since 2010. We believe our customers deserve a high return on investment. We do our best to deliver that through Summit’s Three Quality Promises.

Friendly, knowledgeable wine & spirits lovers

Our clients trust the Summit Brand Building Team to be the face of their brand in the stores.We honor that trust by recruiting the most friendly and knowledgeable wine and spirits enthusiasts we can find. Inviting customers into a brand's story with an enticing question such as, "Would you like to try an affordable French Sauvignon Blanc made from organically grown grapes?" gets high numbers of customers to our tables. Summit will enthusiastically greet and engage with as many people as possible and promote your brand like it’s our very own.

High performance standards

Our clients trust the Summit Brand Building Team to hit key performance indicators like high customer counts and bottle sales. Summit tracks these numbers weekly and pays bonuses to our brand builders who convert at least 25% of sippers into paying customers. We encourage our brand builders to set goals for customer counts and bottle sales for each event and to deliberately work toward them. Summit will set measureable goals for each event, monitor our progress, and reward team members who excel.

Ongoing professional development

Our clients trust the Summit Brand Building Team to enhance their brand’s reputation. Conducting high-quality consumer tastings requires that people have been properly trained on the products and the brand story. No winery or distillery should ever have to work with a company that uses unqualified people that they have told: “Google the winery before you go.” Summit will provide ongoing, targeted professional development for the Summit Brand Building Team because your brand deserves to be represented in the most professional manner.
