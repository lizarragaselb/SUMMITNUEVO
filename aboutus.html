About Us

Many wine and spirits brands struggle to establish and then maintain brand awareness. We have been solving that problem for our clients since 2010 by sharing product information and pouring free samples. That's the easy part. What sets Summit apart from our competitors is our expertise in telling each brand's story and closing sales. Let the Summit Brand Building Team put the spotlight on your brands, so you can focus on doing what you do best: making high-quality wine and spirits.

Summit's High-Quality Promises

Summit has conducted over 12,000 wine and spirits tastings since 2010. We believe our customers deserve a high ROI from their tastings. We do our best to deliver that with Summit's High-Quality Promises.

Friendly, knowledgeable wine & spirits lovers

Our clients trust the Summit Brand Building Team to be the face of their brand in the stores. We honor that trust by recruiting the most friendly and knowledgeable wine and spirits enthusiasts we can find. Inviting customers into a brand's story with an enticing question such as, "Would you like to try an affordable French Sauvignon Blanc made from organically grown grapes?" gets high numbers of customers to our tables. Summit will enthusiastically greet and engage with as many people as possible and promote your brand like it’s our very own.

High performance standards

Our clients trust the Summit Brand Building Team to consistently achieve high customer and bottle counts. Summit tracks these numbers and pays bonuses to our brand builders who convert at least 25% of "tasting customers" into "paying customers." We encourage our brand builders to set goals for each event and to work toward them. Summit will set measurable goals for each event, monitor our progress, and reward team members who excel.

Ongoing professional development

Our clients trust the Summit Brand Building Team to build their brand's reputation. Conducting high-quality consumer tastings requires training on the beverage's features and benefits and on the brand story. No brand should ever have to work with a company whose approach to training is to "Google the brand before you get to the store." Summit will provide ongoing, targeted professional development that enhances our performance and leads to stronger sales.
