Services

Summit has conducted over 12,000 wine and spirits tastings since 2010. We believe our customers deserve a high ROI from their tastings. We do our best to deliver that with Summit's High-Quality Promises.

Consumer Wine & Spirits Tastings

Our team of wine and spirits lovers has a reputation for conducting five-star consumer tastings that build brand awareness and drive sales. Many of our brand builders take wine country vacations or visit distilleries and breweries to gain knowledge and tasting experience. Some take wine classes or pursue professional certifications. "Summit’s people read wine magazines and join wine clubs". As you may have guessed, Summit’s “secret sauce” is our team’s passion for learning about wine and spirits. Schedule a call to learn how Summit can help you spread the good word about your wine or spirits brand!

Corporate Events

Speak with Summit’s founder, Certified Wine Educator Eric Henry, about developing a theme-based corporate event to show your customers or your employees how much you appreciate them. Summit has designed events with themes like “Women Winemakers from Around the World” and “A Wine Tour of the Americas.”Schedule a call to get inspired and get a quote.

Private Events

If you need coverage at a country club or a wine festival, we have experienced brand builders who will dive into your brand's story and share it with as many guests as possible. Certified Wine Educator Eric Henry also designs private events based on each client's specific needs. Schedule a call to get inspired and to get a quote.
